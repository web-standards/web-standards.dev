Josh Comeau walks through the CSS Anchor Positioning API, which lets tooltips and dropdowns attach to a target element. He covers `anchor-name`, `position-anchor`, and `position-area` for placement, `position-try-fallbacks` to reposition when space runs out, and anchored container queries to flip carets. A `flip-block` keyword adds cross-browser support, with `@supports` and a polyfill for older browsers.
