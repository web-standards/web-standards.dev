Carmen Ansio shows how SVG filter primitives recreate historical printing effects on web typography: `feMorphology` draws true outer strokes, stacked `feOffset` layers extrude letters into 3D, `feSpecularLighting` mimics light on raised type, and `feDisplacementMap` with `feTurbulence` distorts text organically. She builds gilded initials, chromatic splits, neon glow, and gooey letter merging, with notes on sRGB blending and performance.
