Jen Simmons introduces the `:open` pseudo-class for styling open `<details>`, `<dialog>`, `<select>`, and `<input>` elements, the `element-scoped` keyword for `random()`, `color-interpolation` with `linearRGB` for SVG gradients, `ToggleEvent.source` for the popover API, and the new Origin API for structured origin comparisons. Plus 63 bug fixes across SVG, WebRTC, networking, editing, anchor positioning, and scroll-driven animations.
